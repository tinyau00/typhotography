Hi, I’m Tin Yau, a passionate photographer with a lifelong love for capturing the world through the lens. My journey with photography began in childhood, and over time, it has grown into so much more than just a hobby – it’s a way of life. Photography allows me to freeze fleeting moments in time, preserve emotions, and tell stories that words alone could never express. Through my camera, I’ve discovered unique angles, hidden details, and perspectives that have changed the way I see the world. It’s a constant reminder to appreciate the beauty in the everyday and the extraordinary. Currently, I’m enriching my skills through professional photography courses, which have deepened my understanding of light, composition, and storytelling. These experiences have only strengthened my belief that photography is not just about taking pictures – it’s about creating art that resonates and connects with people. Thank you for visiting my site! Feel free to explore my portfolio, and don’t hesitate to reach out if you’d like to collaborate or discuss your next project. I’d love to hear from you!
